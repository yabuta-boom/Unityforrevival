Welcome to Our Website

At UNITY FOR REVIVAL, our mission is clear: to focus every eye on the crucified Christ. We are committed to bringing spiritual renewal to Christian high school students, empowering them to live out their faith with conviction. Through gospel-centered teaching and outreach, we aim to inspire all believers to share the transformative message of Jesus with the world. Join us as we seek to preach the gospel to all creation, bringing reformation and hope to those who believe in Christ. Together, we will advance His kingdom, one life at a time.

Mission

Our mission is to focus every eye on the crucified Christ, fulfilling God's plan to spread His love and truth to the world. We are dedicated to wrapping the world in Christ, empowering high school and college students with the Holy Spirit, and guiding them toward spiritual reformation. Through the gospel, we aim to inspire transformation, renewal, and a deeper relationship with Christ in every believer.

Vision

Our vision is to see a generation of high school and college students radically transformed by the power of the gospel. We envision a world where every young believer is equipped with the Holy Spirit, filled with bold faith, and committed to living out the message of Christ. Our goal is to witness a global movement of reformation that brings Christ's light to every corner of the earth, drawing all people to the saving grace of His sacrifice on the cross.

Values

1. Christ-Centered:

We place Jesus at the heart of everything, honoring His sacrifice and transformative power.

2. Reformation:

We seek spiritual renewal through the Holy Spirit, bringing lasting change to individuals and communities.

3. Empowerment:

We equip students with the gospel and the Holy Spirit, empowering them to live out their faith boldly.

4. Global Outreach:

We are dedicated to sharing the gospel worldwide, especially with high school and college students.

5. Discipleship:

We focus on nurturing spiritual growth, guiding believers to follow Christ and spread His love.

6. Community:

We build strong, supportive communities where believers grow together in faith.
